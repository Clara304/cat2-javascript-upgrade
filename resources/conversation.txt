I spoke with the owner of La Casserole Restaurant.
The restaurant serves a variety of meals including grilled chicken, fish, meat dishes, snacks, and beverages. 
The main goal of the website  is to showcase the restaurant, present its menu, highlight popular dishes, and provide contact information for customers.
The target audience includes families, students, workers, and anyone looking for quality meals at the restaurant.
The owner preferred a professional and welcoming design using colors that match the restaurant branding. The website should allow visitors to easily view menu categories, discover the restaurant's specialties, and find contact details.
