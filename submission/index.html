Authentic Congolese & International Cuisine

Welcome to La Casserole Restaurant, a destination where culinary tradition meets modern hospitality. We are proud to serve a diverse menu inspired by the rich flavors of Congolese cuisine alongside carefully selected international favorites.

Whether you are joining us for a family gathering, a business lunch, a celebration with friends, or simply a delicious meal after a long day, our team is committed to providing an unforgettable dining experience.

From freshly grilled meats and traditional vegetable dishes to flavorful fish specialties and refreshing beverages, every meal is prepared with care, passion, and attention to detail.

Chef's Specialities

These signature dishes represent the very best of La Casserole Restaurant. Recommended by our chefs and loved by our customers, these meals showcase authentic flavors, premium ingredients, and exceptional preparation.

Ntaba Choma

Expertly grilled goat meat marinated in a blend of traditional spices and herbs. Served with your choice of accompaniment and prepared to perfection.

Liboke ya Ngolo

A traditional Congolese fish dish prepared using time-honored techniques that preserve flavor and texture while highlighting natural ingredients.

Porc Choma

Tender grilled pork seasoned with our special house blend and served fresh from the grill. One of our most popular dishes among returning customers.
